# Regulated Categories

## Health

- Highest scrutiny for medical, supplement, and wellness claims.
- Required disclaimers and no unsubstantiated cure/treatment claims.
- Hidden disclaimer checks (e.g., display:none) apply.

## Finance

- Loans, credit, investment, and crypto fall under finance rules.
- Risk disclaimers and no guaranteed-return language.
- Domain denylist may include known scam or unlicensed operators.

## Dating and Gambling

- Age and jurisdiction restrictions must be respected.
- No false promises about outcomes (e.g., "guaranteed matches," "win every time").

## General

- General ads still must not be misleading, must not use prohibited phrases, and must not use redirect/cloaking to evade review.

## Cross-Category Rules

- Redirect chain length and domain consistency are evaluated across categories.
- Hidden text heuristics (e.g., font-size:0, visibility:hidden) are checked where HTML snapshot is available.
