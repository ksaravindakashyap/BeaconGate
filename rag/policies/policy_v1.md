# BeaconGate Ad Policy v1

## Scope

This policy applies to all advertising content and landing experiences submitted for review. Advertisers must comply with category-specific rules and general prohibitions.

---

## Health Claims

- Health-related ads must not make unsubstantiated medical or therapeutic claims.
- Claims such as "cures," "treats," or "prevents" disease require appropriate disclaimers and evidence.
- Supplements and wellness products must not imply they replace prescribed medication or medical advice.
- Ads must include a clear disclaimer where required (e.g., "Consult your doctor," "Not intended to diagnose or treat").
- Testimonials in health ads must not imply typical results without substantiation.

---

## Finance Claims

- Financial products and investment ads must not guarantee returns or downplay risk.
- Crypto and high-risk investment ads must include risk disclaimers.
- Loan and credit offers must clearly state terms, APR, and eligibility where applicable.
- Impersonation of banks or financial institutions is prohibited.

---

## Misleading Claims

- Ad copy and landing content must not make false or deceptive claims.
- Before-and-after or comparative claims require substantiation.
- Pricing and offer terms displayed in the ad must match the landing page.
- Omission of material information that would affect user decision is prohibited.

---

## Redirect and Cloaking

- Landing URL must not redirect users to a different domain without clear justification.
- Cloaking (showing different content to reviewers vs. users) is prohibited.
- Multiple redirects that obscure the final destination may trigger review.
- The final landing domain should be consistent with the advertised product or service.

---

## Disclaimers

- Health category ads must include at least one approved disclaimer phrase (e.g., "Consult your doctor," "Not medical advice").
- Financial ads must include risk and/or regulatory disclaimers where required.
- Disclaimers must be visible and not hidden (e.g., via small font, low contrast, or display:none).

---

## Prohibited Phrases

- The following phrases or close variants are prohibited in ad copy: "guaranteed cure," "no risk," "get rich quick," "act now or miss out," and similar high-pressure or unsubstantiated language.
- Hate speech, discrimination, and illegal content are prohibited across all categories.

---

## Evidence and Review

- Submitted evidence (screenshot, HTML snapshot, redirect chain) is used for deterministic rule checks and reviewer assessment.
- Policy and precedent retrieval is advisory; the reviewer remains responsible for the final decision.
